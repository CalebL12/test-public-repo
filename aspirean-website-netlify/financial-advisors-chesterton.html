Who we work with in Northwest Indiana

Business owners & founders

We work with business owners preparing for a sale, buyout, or recapitalization. Planning typically starts 1 to 5 years before the event and can include QSBS §1202 exclusion planning, trust structuring, and coordination with your banker and attorney throughout the deal.

Equity-compensated executives

We work with executives holding concentrated company stock or equity compensation, including those who commute to Chicago for work. This includes 10b5-1 plan design, staged diversification, and coordinated tax planning around vesting and sale timing.

Widows & families

We work with widows and families taking on financial responsibility for the first time, whether after a loss or to make sure both spouses are equally prepared. This includes estate settlement, income planning, and rebuilding a financial plan around your current situation.

Frequently asked questions

Do you have a minimum asset level?

Yes. We best serve individuals with at least $1 million in investable assets, including 401(k) plan assets, or more complex financial situations. Our minimum annual fee is $7,500 for clients below that threshold.

How are you compensated?

We are a fee-only Registered Investment Advisor and a fiduciary, paid via a percentage of the assets we manage.

Do you work with clients outside Northwest Indiana?

Yes. We serve clients nationally, with additional offices in Mill Valley, CA, and St. Joseph, MI.
